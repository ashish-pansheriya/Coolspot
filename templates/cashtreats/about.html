Our Story

CashTreats began in 2020. After years in the entertainment industry, we realized that it was near impossible for the average Jane or Joe to create their own Events or website.

Traditional web hosting services were simply too complicated, time consuming, and expensive to manage.

We created the CashTreats Site with the user's perspective in mind. We wanted to offer a platform that would require wonderful skill to help others. We keep it simple, so users can focus on creating an amazing Events that reflects their brand. Best of all - it's free. You can get online, showcase your brand, or start selling products right away.

After seeing an increased need for ecommerce solutions, we developed one of the only fully-featured, free and commission-free online store, allowing users to launch their online business.

Today, we're proud to empower individuals and small business owners around the world. Everyone deserves a happiness, and we're excited to see what you create.
